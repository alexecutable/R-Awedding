QUESTIONS

  • What is the dress code?

    We are not enforcing a dress code. Come as you are most comfortable, whether that is dressed up or down.

  • Where is the [ceremony] / [reception] taking place?

    Both will be at the same venue, the Beazell Memorial Forest. The brief ceremony will be held outside (under shelter if weather is unyielding), and the reception inside.

  • When should I arrive?

    We recommend you arrive at most 30 minutes before the start time (so, 5:30pm at the earliest). There will be food and drink for anyone arriving early. We will start the (short) ceremony at 6:30.

  • Is there parking at the venue?

    Parking is free but VERY limited. We recommend carpooling wherever possible. If you would like to be picked up from either Corvallis or Independence/Monmouth, please let us know and we can arrange transportation for you.

  • Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

    Yes, but parking is limited, and we have a few friends in wheelchairs, so please note your required accessibility needs in order for us to accomodate everyone.

  • Are the ceremony and reception indoors or outdoors?

    The ceremony will be outdoors, under shelter if necessary. The reception will be indoors, but there is also outdoor space provided at Beazell for your leisure.

  • What happens after the ceremony?

    After the ceremony, there will be a brief interlude for photos, guests may go directly inside after the ceremony where food and drink will be served.

  • Will there be food / drink?

    Yes, we will have food and both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

  • Is there vegan/vegetarian/gluten-free/etc...?

    Yes, we plan to have a variety of food options available. Noting your needs on your RSVP will ensure that we can meet those needs accordingly.

  • Where I should I stay?

    We do not have a hotel block reserved. For out-of-town guests we recommend staying in either Corvallis (if coming from Eugene) or Independence / Monmouth (if coming from Portland). If you need overnight accomodations, please let us know and we can assist you!

  • Are kids welcome?

    Yes! If you are comfortable bringing your children, please feel free! Do note how many are in your party when RSVPing, though.

  • Can I bring a +1?

    Yes, but please note how many persons are in your party when RSVPing, as we have a small venue with limited capacity.

  • Is there a theme?

    No. :)

  • Are you registered?

    We are not requiring or requesting gifts. We will have anonymous tip jars available as well as a card box. For those who insist, we can provide a link to a registry (so we don't end up with 4 toasters :) ).
